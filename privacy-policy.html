Privacy Policy for MyDiet

Last updated: June 2025

What This Policy Covers

This privacy policy explains how MyDiet collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you create an account:

  • Your name and email address
  • Basic info like age, height, weight, and health goals

When you use the app:

  • The foods you log and your nutrition data
  • Photos you take of food (for scanning)
  • Your weight and progress tracking
  • How you use the app (what features you use most)

Technical information:

  • Your device type and operating system
  • App crashes and errors (to fix problems)

How We Use Your Information

To provide the app's features:

  • Track your nutrition and calories
  • Scan and identify foods
  • Show your progress over time
  • Give you personalized recommendations

To improve the app:

  • Fix bugs and add new features
  • Understand which features are most helpful
  • Provide customer support

Information We Share

With other services (to make the app work):

  • Food databases to get nutrition information
  • AI services to identify food in photos
  • Google Sign-In for secure login

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your health data with advertisers
  • Give your information to other apps or companies for their own use

Legal requirements:

  • We may share information if required by law
  • To protect our users' safety

How We Protect Your Information

  • Your data is encrypted and stored securely
  • Only authorized people can access your information
  • We regularly check our security systems
  • Food photos are processed as securely as possible

Your Control Over Your Data

You can:

  • View and update your profile information anytime
  • Delete your account and data through app settings
  • Export your data if you want to use it elsewhere
  • Turn off usage tracking in settings

How long we keep your data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • When you delete your account, we delete your personal data
  • Anonymous usage data may be kept to improve the app

Children's Privacy

MyDiet is not for children under 13. If you're a parent and think your child has used our app, please contact us and we'll delete their information.

Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this policy occasionally
  • We'll notify you of important changes through the app
  • The "Last updated" date shows when we made changes
